About me

Hi, I’m Matt. I was born in the early 80’s in Margate, a seaside town in the county of Kent in the UK. Currently I live in Sevenoaks; still in Kent, but closer to London; with my wife, daughter and our cat.

I hold a BEng (hons) in Computer Systems Engineering from the University of Kent (although it was called University of Kent at Canterbury when I studied there).

I also hold a MSc in Broadband and Mobile Communication Networks from the same University after my undergraduate supervisor applied for EPSRC funding on my behalf (and without my knowledge, it was a pleasant surprise).

I started my career as a programmer straight away after leaving University. After a couple of years as a junior Delphi, Visual Basic and C# programmer I changed direction. I found Ruby in 2007 via a junior Systems Administrator position at a startup based in London.

I moved from SysOps into Development in 2008 and I’ve been writing Ruby for the last 11 years in various forms.

As of November 2018 I am a Senior Developer at Shopify. Shopify are based in Canada and I work remotely from my home in the UK. Working with a 5 hour time difference is a new experience for me, but

Previously I have worked at FutureLearn, OnTheBeach and Reevoo

When I’m not working I enjoy music, both playing and listening. As my pithy slogan intimates, I’m not very good; predominantly because I don’t do enough deliberate practice, but it makes me happy.

I have pretty varied music tastes. I’ve always been a metalhead, ever since I taped my first Metallica songs off the radio at age 7, but I’ve also got a lot of time for electronic music, UK Hip-Hop and Grime, Chiptune, Post-rock, Screamo, Math rock, Classical music and whatever else takes my fancy.

I enjoy anime, manga and video games. My favourite console is my PlayStation Vita, the most underrated handheld console ever released in my view. I have the originally released version (with the OLED screen). It’s a Shame that Sony are discontinuing it in 2019. One day I’d like to get into PSVita development.
