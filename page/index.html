About

2018-07-21 ::
Hi, I’m Matt. I was born in the early 80’s in Margate, a seaside town in the county of Kent in the UK. Currently I live in Sevenoaks; still in Kent, but closer to London; with my wife, two kids, and our cat. I hold a BEng (hons) in Computer Systems Engineering from the University of Kent. I also hold a MSc in Broadband and Mobile Communication Networks from the same University after my undergraduate supervisor applied for EPSRC funding on my behalf (and without my knowledge), it was a pleasant surprise, that enabled me to afford to continue to study.
